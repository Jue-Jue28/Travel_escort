Welcome to Daung Lann Gyi

About this resturant

If you want to try Burmese transitional cuisines, I suggest you eat in Daung Lann Gyi restaurant as it’s famous for Myanmar traditional recipes. It really suits you if you want to have a full Myanmar experience. Both exterior and interior of the restaurant is decorated with Burmese traditional aesthetics and Burmese Orchestra is also played peacefully so you can explore the real beauty of Myanmar while having a meal.
Service options: Dine-in, Takeaway, Delivery
