Welcome to Farm House

About this resturant

This is one of the most popular food around in Kalaw, especially in Shan state. Since 1946, it is really famous in Kalaw also Mandalay too. This recipe is quite simple to do and don’t need too much ingredients. For Shan noodles, it has two types. First one is look like salad and the other one is for soup. In this two, all of the ingredients are same but noodles are different. For soup, it made with (Sensee) which are sticky and flat. Noodles which are round, thin and not sticky used for soup. Shan noodles make with chicken or pork cooked in tomatoes sauce. The taste is depend on sauce that is control and rise up the noodles. It match together with pickled mustard green (mon nyin jin). Shan noodles are very delicious and try to get the taste for life.

Welcome to Black sesame seed and sticky rice cake (khor poat)

About this resturant

Khor Poat is one kind of traditional snack that is mix with sticky rice and black sesame. It can give many kind of nutrition and support healthy bones cause of black sesame. It has been for more than 100 years and still fresh now. It is really interesting to see how they prepare it. They place the cooked rice into a stone “bowl” situated on ground and use wooden machine to smash and mix it, and making a dough and put some oil. The tastiest ways to have it fried and barbequed. This is one of nice present when came back from Kalaw. It is selling by wrapping in banana leaf so it doesn’t dry easily and can buy anywhere of Kalaw.
